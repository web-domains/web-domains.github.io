Our Services

We will provide you to quality products with our multidisciplinary team with decades of experience.

T​errestrial Surveying

Our experienced team is ready to measure with drones and state-of-the-art GNSS equipment for construction, mining, infrastructure, road and bridge works.

3D City Modelling

We provide quality 3D City Models that we create with Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing discipline applications using LiDAR technology.

Geospatial Asset Management

An application that will allow you to manage your assets on a 2D or 3D map based on CesiumJS. In this way, you can get efficiency and savings by assigning your teams to the assets on time and at low cost.
